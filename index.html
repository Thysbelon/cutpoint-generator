Cutpoint Group Frequency Distribution Table Diagram Generator

Manually counting data points to create a diagram for a Statistics class assignment is monotonous and mindless work. I was able to find generators for limit group frequency distribution diagrams and stem and leaf diagrams, but I had to create my own cutpoint group frequency distribution diagram generator for continuous data A.K.A. decimal numbers. When entering data, please be consistent with your delimiters. If there is a newline for every number, that's okay; but if there's a newline for every eight numbers, the generator won't work.
